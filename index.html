Two Markers

Show two markers in one overlay: https://obs-overlay-help.vercel.app/two-marker.html?streamerId=437343696&otherStreamerId=753285921 Replace streamerId and otherStreamerId with the two Twitch ids.

3D Streets

Follow the streamer with 3D plain buildings. https://obs-overlay-help.vercel.app/3d-streets.html?key=&fullscreen=1 Replace pullKey with your RealtimeIRL pull key.

Rainview

See radar data on the overlay, you may need to adjust zoom to see what you want using the &zoom=5 parameter. https://obs-overlay-help.vercel.app/rainview.html?key=&fullscreen=1 Replace pullKey with your RealtimeIRL pull key.

Citibike

Made to follow Terry Barentsen's bike angel challenge. https://obs-overlay-help.vercel.app/citibike.html This is probably not very useful for others.

StreamElements Overlays

StreamElements iFrame seems to block parts of the overlays hosted by RealtimeIRL, however, you can achieve the same by directly having the code there, you can see some examples in this link Note: You will need to provide your Realtime pull key and other keys depending on the overlay.
